Rockrolling the world - 70 countries in 24 hours

Overview

howareyoufeeling.xyz, a fun website I launched in July 2024, was used by 1.5k people in 360 cities across 70 countries in the first 24 hours.

The Origin Story

I used to have discussions with one of my best friends at work about everything, but we kept revisiting WWE and Desi Hip Hop. During one of these discussion, we came up with the idea to rockroll people. Bought the domain instantly, but we all know how most side projects go.

Finally decided to complete it and build it 2 years later. And here it is.

How did it grow?

The experience is fun and has virality built in. Try it out yourself. I also DMed everyone I know who I thought would have a slight interest in this fun, little meme of mine.
